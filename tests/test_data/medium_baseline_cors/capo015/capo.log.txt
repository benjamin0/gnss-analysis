                              : plug that is welded into the mast.  The mast is
                              : fastened to a masonry building via a set of 2 in
                              : slotted strut channel, SS bolts and 3" pipe
                              : clamps.  The upper and lower strut channel are
                              : mounted horizontally and are affixed to the

                              : hollow set wall anchors.  (2) 3" SS pipe clamp
                              : straps are used to secure the mast to the upper
                              : and lower strut channel.  A vertical section of
                              : strut channel is located directly behind the
                              : mast to act as a spacer for the (3) additional
                              : SS bolts that pass thru the mast assembly and
                              : into (3) additional anchors.  The vertical
                              : section of strut channel secured to the upper
                              : and lower sections via (4) tee welds.
